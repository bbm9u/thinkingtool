Thinking Tool

The Thinking Tool provides a template for focusing a research assignment through the brainstorming of ideas, keywords, and other terminology related to a topic. The objective is the generation of searchable keywords that may be used to gather resources.

Step 1

What is your assignment?

How long is your paper? How many sources do you need? What kind?

Step 2

What do you want to write about?

This doesn't have to be your final choice of topic. It's a place to start.

Step 3

Build a Concept Cloud

Write down anything related to your main idea. Anything and everything! (Use Wikipedia for help)

Step 4

Narrow Your Search Terms

Choose 2 to 4 keywords from Step #3 to start the search process. Remember: Add or subtract keywords to get different results. You may find that you need to start over with a new main idea.
